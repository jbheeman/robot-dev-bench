Key Metrics

Smoothness (LDLJ)
Log Dimensionless Jerk. Measures movement fluidity by integrating squared jerk over the trajectory. Higher (closer to 0) indicates smoother movement.
--
Smoothness (SPARC)
Spectral Arc Length. Measures smoothness in the frequency domain. Less sensitive to noise than LDLJ. Higher values indicate smoother movement.
--
Symmetry Index
Measures left-right joint symmetry by comparing movement variance between opposing sides. 0% means perfectly symmetrical, higher values indicate asymmetry (up to 200%).
-- %
Periodicity Score
Measures gait regularity using autocorrelation. A score of 1.0 indicates perfect repetition of the movement cycle.
--
ROM Utilisation
Range of Motion Utilisation. The mean angular displacement across all active joints. Indicates how dynamically the robot's capabilities are being used.
